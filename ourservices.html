Our Services

We service all makes and models!

We handle all types of vacuum cleaners. Here are a few of the brands that we work on:
-Kirby
-Riccar
-Hoover
-Bissell
-Rainbow
-Panasonic
-Simplicity
-Electrolux
And any other machine that you can find!

Standard Tune-up and Service

Our standard tune-up and service changes from machine to machine, but usually consists of cleaning the rughead, cleaning the roller, cleaning the motor compartment, checking the motorbrushes, cleaning the armature, cleaning the bag compartment (or if your machine is bagless, cleaning the dirt compartment), replacing the belt, and other standart maintenance.

Replacement Parts

We will have most Kirby and Riccar parts in stock. We have huge inventory of parts for other brand machines, as well.

Belts and Bags

We belts and bags for most machines. If you want to know if we carry your specific belt or bag, you can call in advance and we will be happy to answer any of your questions.
